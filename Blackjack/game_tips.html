Game Tips

  • Always assume the dealer's face-down card is a 10.
  • If you have a total of 12-16 and the dealer shows a 7 or higher, consider hitting.
  • If you have a total of 17 or higher, it's usually best to stand.
  • Use basic strategy charts to improve your chances of winning.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you play, the better you'll get.

Good Luck!
